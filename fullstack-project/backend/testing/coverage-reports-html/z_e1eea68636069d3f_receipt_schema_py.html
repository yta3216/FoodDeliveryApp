2This module defines the schema for order receipts. 

3A receipt is created when a customer requests a cost breakdown of their cart 

4before submitting payment. It is saved independently and referenced by the

18 * **menu_item_id** (int): the identifier of this item on its associated menu

31class Receipt(BaseModel): 

32 """ 

33 **Defines the attributes of a receipt. A receipt is a priced snapshot of a 

34 customer's cart created before payment is submitted. It is saved to storage 

35 and referenced by the order after a successful payment.** 

36 

37 Attributes: 

38 * **id** (int): the identifier of the receipt 

39 * **customer_id** (str): the identifier of the customer who requested the receipt 

40 * **restaurant_id** (int): the identifier of the restaurant being ordered from 

41 * **items** (list[ReceiptItem]): priced line items from the cart at time of receipt creation 

42 * **subtotal** (float): total price of items before tax and delivery fee 

43 * **tax** (float): tax applied to the order 

44 * **delivery_fee** (float): fee for delivering the order 

45 * **total** (float): subtotal + tax + delivery_fee 

46 """ 

47 id: int 

48 customer_id: str 

49 restaurant_id: int 

50 items: list[ReceiptItem] = Field(default_factory=list) 

51 subtotal: float = 0.0 

52 tax: float = 0.0 

53 delivery_fee: float = 0.0 

54 total: float = 0.0
